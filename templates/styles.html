ðŸŽ¨ Understanding Style Controls

Use these sliders to enhance your image and match your desired edit style. Here's what each one does:

  • Brightness: Lightens or darkens the image overall.
  • Contrast: Increases difference between dark and light areas.
  • Saturation: Boosts or tones down color intensity.
  • Hue: Rotates the color spectrum to shift tones.
  • Gamma: Adjusts midtone brightness without blowing out shadows/highlights.
  • Temperature: Warms (orange) or cools (blue) the image color tone.
  • Tint: Adds green or magenta tones to balance the image feel.
